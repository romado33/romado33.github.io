Approach

Keep the “Technical Poet” voice, but after proof.

How I work

  • Start with user intent: what someone is trying to do, not what the system is.
  • Show the happy path: quickstart first, depth second.
  • Make failures legible: error meanings, troubleshooting, and next actions.
  • Ship like product: versioning, feedback loops, and measurable outcomes.

Why “Technical Poet”

Good documentation translates between worlds: engineers, implementers, and end users. I aim for clarity that respects both the system and the human reading it—precise, concise, and usable.
